About Us

Our Story

Shane Salon has been a trusted destination in Westwood, Los Angeles for clients who want expert haircuts, color, blowouts, and highlights. We focus on personalized service, honest recommendations, and results that keep clients coming back for years.

Our Philosophy

We believe in combining skill with a genuine commitment to each client. Whether you're in for a quick cut, a full color transformation, or a blowout that lasts, we aim to make you look and feel your best in a welcoming, professional environment.

What We Offer

We specialize in haircuts, color, highlights, and blowouts. Our team has an eye for what suits you—natural, flattering color and styles that work for your life. Plus, we offer free parking validation and a convenient Westwood location.
